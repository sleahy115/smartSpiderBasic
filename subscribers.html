Subscriber's Agreement

The Bradley Connection, LLC/DBA Smartspider.net has been organized to offer a website, listing the names of New Mexico consultants in three primary categories: 1. Services to businesses, 2.Services to not-for-profit organizations and 3. Lifestyle/Enhancing services

The following Site Members (hereafter "Subscribers") Agreement will apply to all consultants/subscribers applying for membership and inclusion on Smartspider.net. Please read the following carefully. By completing the following application, you agree to abide by and comply with this Subscribers Agreement and warrant that all information supplied in your application is a true and accurate reflection of your qualifications and experience as a consultant.

  1. QUALIFYING TO BE ON THE SITE
    • Are able to provide information as listed in the application that outlines education, seminars, professional organization, references and/or other special training, qualifying you for the categories in which you wish to be listed.
    • Have worked as a recognized consultant.
    • Agree that you will not use Smartspider.net for multilevel marketing.
    • Agree that you will not use Smartspider.net to sell products/goods. Exception to this rule will be books and other material used in performance of contractual obligations and/or in the teaching of classes.
    • Maintain the highest professional standards and conduct with all clients.
  2. SMARTSPIDER.NET WILL
    • Maintain a web site with names and qualifications of all subscribers of Smartspider.net. Each paid subscriber will select the categories in which they will be listed with no restriction as to number of categories or selections and may be in as many categories as they choose.
    • Provide regularly scheduled networking opportunities for subscribers and their guests. These will be publicized to the general business community, giving all subscribers access to new clients.
    • Engage in marketing activities, driving New Mexico business, not-for-profit communities and private citizens to Smartspider.net when seeking a professional consultant.
    • Actively seek association and alliances with professional organizations/associations.
    • Utilize unique website capabilities to drive clients to blogs and other informational opportunities of subscribers.
  3. SMARTSPIDER.NET WILL NOT
    • Make suggestions or recommendations to clients as to professional services. Endorse any subscriber, their services or activities.
    • Engage in or be a party to neither monetary nor other disputes between client and subscriber.
    • Charge a fee to any client or visitor to the site.
    • All contractual agreements and arrangements are between Subscriber and site user/client. The Bradley connection, LLC dba/Smartspider.net will not be responsible for any contractual arrangement between subscribers and clients.
  4. FEE STRUCTURE
    • A subscriber fee of $295, plus GRT, for one calendar year will apply and begin on the date of application acceptance and will be in force for twelve continuous months.
    • A fee reduction may apply for members of affiliated associations and organizations. Please inquire if you belong to professional associations and use the provided code on the application.

All Fees are due at the time of application and must be paid by credit card and online. Application must be completed online and by the applicant. Applicant will be given or select a personal code for use in updating personal information. Applicant will have complete discretion as to categories in which he/she wishes to be listed, listing of qualifications, references, web site listing along with blogs, other social media and contact information.

THE BRADLEY CONNECTION, LLC/DBA SMARTSPIDER.NET reserves the right to modify these regulations from time to time without prior notice to, or approval of, subscribers to Smartspider.net; however no such modification or restatement of the subscribers agreement will be binding on subscribers until posted to the website or will operate to increase any subscriber's fee during the term of any subscriber's existing subscription to the website.

Entire Subscribers Agreement. These rules and regulations comprise all of the agreement that govern SmartSpider.net and subscribers to the website and supersede all prior agreements of Smartspider.net.
